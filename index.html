Kohortz For Teams

Find and team up with your soon-to-be best friends

Kohortz is a platform built for the rapidly growing global community of freelancers, independent contractors and full-time part-timers. Kohortz enables them to organize themselves into teams so they can provide a more complete line of services to clients, but also ensure that they (actually) like those they work with and are able to cultivate a real team culture. If the gig economy has taught us anything it is that flexibility and freedom are highly desirable, but the flexibility and freedom of independent contracting often comes with a loss of community. There is a loss of shared accomplishment, accountability,  and distributed responsibility – in short you have no one to pop bottles with, call you out on your crap or offer understanding shoulder to cry on. To solve for this, Kohortz - at its heart - is a hub of fun, networking, assembly, and productivity in this new freelancing/gig world. One can think of Kohortz as the result of a freaky four-way between Gigster (individual role processing), Match.com (team matching algorithm), LinkedIn (business/team social site), and Etsy (business/team storefronts) - with an open API that allows for all kinds of team focused add-ons, resources, tools and partnerships.

Kohortz For Employers

Someone has to pay for the fun...

Clients love Kohortz as well, because they are able to hire based on projects, timelines and strategy - without the headache of wrangling and managing each individual freelancer. Instead of wasting time searching through pages and pages of freelancers or companies; only to be burned by a lack of responsiveness or poor deliverables, Kohortz will match a client’s request with a curated list of teams that meet the requested criteria. Want to go out on a limb,  pay less, and get more...“interesting” work?  Change your request criteria to match your propensity for risk. Kohortz houses tons of young and hungry teams ready to prove themselves. Want to find an established team with a proven track record, consistently on time deliverables and always incredible work? You may have to pay for it and wait in line, but we have the data to back up the fact that they won’t let you down.
